About JPCS

Building a community of passionate tech enthusiasts and future innovators

Our Mission

To foster excellence in computer science education and empower students to become innovative leaders in the technology industry through collaborative learning, mentorship, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To be the premier student organization that bridges academic learning with real-world technology applications, creating a vibrant ecosystem where students can develop technical skills, build meaningful connections, and make a positive impact on society.

Our Values

Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
Collaboration: We believe in the power of teamwork and collective growth.
Innovation: We embrace new ideas and technologies.
Community: We are committed to giving back and supporting each other.

What We Do

Programs

Educational Programs

We organize workshops, seminars, and training sessions covering various aspects of computer science and technology.

Events

Competitions

We host programming competitions and hackathons to challenge students and showcase their skills.

Outreach

Community Outreach

We engage with the broader community through tech literacy programs and volunteer initiatives.

Connect

Networking

We facilitate connections between students, professionals, and industry leaders.

Our Journey

A brief history of JPCS milestones

2010

Foundation

JPCS was founded by a group of passionate computer science students with the vision of creating a supportive tech community.

2013

First Hackathon

Organized our first major hackathon with over 100 participants, marking our entry into competitive tech events.

2016

National Recognition

Received the "Outstanding Student Organization" award and expanded our reach to collaborate with other universities nationwide.

2020

Digital Transformation

Successfully pivoted to online events during the pandemic, reaching more students than ever before through virtual workshops and webinars.

2024

500+ Members

Reached a milestone of 500+ active members and launched industry partnership programs with leading tech companies.

2026

Present Day

Continuing to innovate and grow, with plans for international collaborations and cutting-edge tech initiatives.
